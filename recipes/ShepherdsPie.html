Shepherd's Pie

Crescent roll chicken pot pie braid

A classic recipe for Shepher's Pie containing peas, carrots

corn, beef, potatoes, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Cans of peas, carrots, and corn

  • 1/2 lb. ground beef

  • 2 cups cheddar cheese

  • Mashed Potatoes

    Steps

  1. Make mashed potatoes from mix or scratch

  2. Brown beef in a pan

  3. Create a bottom layer in a pie pan with cooked beef

  4. Add the mixed vegetables for the next layer

  5. Add the mashed potatoes to the top of the vegetables

  6. Top the pie with two cups of shredded cheese

  7. Bake in the oven at 350 degrees for 30 minutes
