Spaghetti

Spaghetti noodles with tomato sauce topped with basil

This recipe uses angel hair noodles and original tomato sauce to create a classic pasta entree.
The noodles and sauce receive additional flavor add-ons at the end of the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Angel hair noodles

  • 1 lb. ground beef

  • 1 jar of tomato sauce

  • basil

  • grated parmesan cheese

    Steps

  1. Boil the noodles in a pot of water

  2. Brown the ground beef in a pan

  3. Drain the water from the noodles

  4. Combine the noodles, beef, and sauce in a pot.

  5. Top servings with basil and parmesan cheese.
