Pot Pie Braid

Crescent roll chicken pot pie braid

This recipe creates a new take on the traditional pot pie. It has the same delicous
fillings such as chicken and vegetables, but uses crescent rolls as the crust.

Ingredients

  • One container of crescent rolls

  • 1/2 lb. chicken breast

  • 1 can of peas and carrots

  • Creamed cheddar cheese

    Steps

  1. Cook chicken breast on stovetop

  2. In a large bowl mix together chicken, vegetables, and creamed cheese

  3. Carefully unroll the crescent rolls and lay them flat on a pan

  4. Add the mixture to the flattended rolls

  5. Fold the flat crescents over the fillings in the shape of a braid

  6. Bake in the oven at 350 degrees for 12 minutes or until crescents are golden brown
